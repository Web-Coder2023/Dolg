Your questions, gently answered

01

Can I have multiple surrogacy journeys happening at the same time?

Yes. This is entirely possible, and we’re equipped to coordinate simultaneous journeys with more than one surrogate for families looking to grow in this way.

02

Can I create embryos in one country and carry out the surrogacy in another?

Yes. We support internationally coordinated care, including embryo creation and shipping across borders for surrogacy in a separate country.

03

Can I use both donor egg and donor sperm?

Yes — in our programs, egg donation must be known, and we help manage the legal, medical, and emotional aspects of combining both donor sources.

04

Can I use a donor from one country and have the embryos shipped elsewhere?

Yes. We arrange the secure shipping of cryopreserved eggs, sperm, or embryos internationally as part of your plan.

05

Can embryos be created locally and then shipped internationally for surrogacy?

Yes. We handle coordination across clinics and borders, ensuring all documentation and logistics are in place.

06

What if I want to bring my baby home as quickly as possible?

Our strong international partnerships allow us to navigate documentation processes with efficiency — giving you access to some of the fastest legal processing timelines available and helping you bring baby home sooner.

Can this include travel to beautiful destinations?

Yes. Your family-building journey can also be a rich, life-enhancing experience in stunning international settings — we coordinate everything for ease, comfort, and peace of mind.

How long does the process take from start to finish?

Timelines vary by journey, but our broad network and streamlined program help most families move forward without unnecessary delays.
